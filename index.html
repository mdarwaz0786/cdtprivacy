Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Code Diffusion Technologies ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal data of users ("you", "your") of the mobile application CDT Portal (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following information from users:

  • Employee name and ID
  • Location data (used only during check-in/check-out)
  • Device information (OS version, model)
  • Time and date of attendance actions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To track and manage employee attendance
  • To verify user location during attendance marking
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To communicate with your employer or HR system

3. Location Permission Disclosure

The app uses foreground location access permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) solely to confirm your location at the time of marking attendance (check-in/check-out). This data is not collected continuously and is not used for background tracking.

The location is accessed only when the user initiates the attendance action, and the app requests permission through a system dialog in compliance with Android guidelines.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • With your employer or HR personnel
  • With service providers under contract who help with our business operations
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process

5. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to secure your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.
